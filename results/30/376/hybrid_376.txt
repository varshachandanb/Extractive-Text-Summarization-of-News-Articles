The Tories said a large chunk of the Budget appeared to have been leaked in what they describe as a "serious breach of Treasury confidentiality". The Lib Dems called for Commons leader Peter Hain to make a statement and said chancellors had resigned over leaks.Raising a point of order, he said it was "the latest in a long line of discourtesies to this House", as well as a breach of confidentiality. Liberal Democrat David Laws said it was a "very serious matter" and said Mr Hain should make a statement on Thursday.
