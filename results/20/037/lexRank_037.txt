Profile: David Miliband  David Miliband's rapid rise through the ranks of government continues with his promotion to Cabinet Office minister.
Elected in a safe Labour seat in 2001 his previous job was school standards minister - a role he won in May 2002.
Prior to the last election he was a key figure in New Labour as the head of the Downing Street policy unit where he was a key member of the manifesto writing team.
